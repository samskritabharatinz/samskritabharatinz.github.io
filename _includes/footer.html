Samskrita Bharati New Zealand

A not-for-profit charitable trust run by volunteers. We belong to the global network of Samskrita Bharati organisations. Our purpose is to promote, preserve and practise Samskritam (Sanskrit) language and its knowledge systems in New Zealand, through classes, discussions, and workshops, both online and in person.
