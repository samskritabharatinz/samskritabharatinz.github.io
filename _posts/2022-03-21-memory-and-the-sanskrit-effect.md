Does learning Sanskrit improve your memory?

Well... research has certainly shown that it does. Dr. James Hartzell, a world-renowned neuroscientist and a Sanskrit learner, coined the term "Sanskrit effect" in the journal *Scientific American*. He noticed that his cognitive functions like memory and decision making were greatly enhanced as a result of learning the Sanskrit language. He then conducted further research with people who have trained in learning Sanskrit texts.

To find out more about his research, you can look up his article in *Scientific American* ("A Neuroscientist Explores the Sanskrit Effect"), the piece in *The Minds Journal*, and his original paper in *NeuroImage* (via ScienceDirect).

Some of us have experienced this personally. A few of my fellow students have observed that it has improved their ability to recollect information faster.

As always, feedback on your experiences is welcome.
