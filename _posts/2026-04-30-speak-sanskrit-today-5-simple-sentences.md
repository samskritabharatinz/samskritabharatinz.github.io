Think Sanskrit is difficult? Let's prove that wrong in 2 minutes. Here are 5 sentences you can start using today:

- **नमस्ते** (Namaste) - Hello
- **भवान् कथम् अस्ति?** (Bhavaan katham asti?) - How are you?
- **अहं कुशलं अस्मि** (Aham kushalam asmi) - I am fine
- **भवतः नाम किम्?** (Bhavataḥ naama kim?) - What is your name?
- **मम नाम जो ब्लोग्ग्स्** (Mama naama Joe Bloggs) - My name is ___

That's it. You've just had your first Sanskrit conversation.

Try this today with a friend or family member.
