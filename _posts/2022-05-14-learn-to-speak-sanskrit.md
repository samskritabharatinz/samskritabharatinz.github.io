SBNZ is happy to announce the 2022 winter class series, starting with a free Sambhashana Shibhiram to introduce Samskrutam to anyone, even with no background at all in Samskrutam or any Indian language.

The Shibhiram, meaning class, will start on 23rd May at 7.30pm over Zoom and run for 10 consecutive evenings, with the exception of Thursday 26th and Sunday 29th May. Please visit our website for more information and to register.

These classes will be followed by Pravesha (entry level), Parichaya (next level) and Shiksha (the 3rd level), with these classes running twice a week starting early June. Fees for Pravesha, Parichaya and Shiksha are NZ$70 each. For each level, classes will be held over Zoom for approximately 6 months, with an examination and certification at the end.

Please support Samskrita Bharati NZ to spread Samskrutam in New Zealand and around the world.
