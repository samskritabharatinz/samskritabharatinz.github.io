Samskrita Bharati New Zealand is happy to announce a free Samskrita Sambhashana Shibiram (Spoken Sanskrit Camp) to introduce Sanskrit to all who are interested in learning the Devabhaashaa. No previous background in Sanskrit is necessary. The content is designed for maximum learning through spoken interaction, following a "Listen, Speak, Read, Write" approach.

At the end of the course, you will be able to converse in simple Sanskrit and gain confidence and motivation to pursue further Sanskrit studies.

The Shibiram will start on 1st October 2025 at 8pm over Zoom, and run every Wednesday for 8 to 10 consecutive weeks. Depending on participation, it could be twice a week for a shorter duration. Please register for this on our [Learn]({{ '/learn/' | relative_url }}) page.

Please note that this is also a pre-requisite to the next step in the Sanskrit studies beginner's course, Pravesha.

Please support Samskrita Bharati New Zealand to spread Samskritam in New Zealand and around the world.
