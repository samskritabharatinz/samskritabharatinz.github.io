Learning Sanskrit doesn't need a classroom. Start in your kitchen! Here are some fun words:

- **जलम्** (Jalam) - Water
- **क्षीरम्** (Ksheeram) - Milk
- **फलम्** (Phalam) - Fruit
- **शाकम्** (Shaakam) - Vegetables
- **अन्नम्** (Annam) - Cooked rice
- **भोजनम्** (Bhojanam) - Meals

Now try saying these simple sentences:

- **अहं फलम् खादामि** (Aham Phalam Khaadaami) - I eat fruit
- **अहं जलं पिबामि** (Aham Jalam Pibaami) - I drink water
- **अहं शाकम् पचामि** (Aham Shaakam Pachaami) - I cook vegetables
