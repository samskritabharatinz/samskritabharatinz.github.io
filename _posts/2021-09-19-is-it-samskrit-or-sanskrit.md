Many of you will be familiar with Sanskrit. And you may be puzzled why this site spells it Samskrit.

Well! *sáṃskṛta-* is a compound word consisting of *sáṃ* ('together, good, well, perfected') and *kṛta-* ('made, formed, work'). When joined together, it forms *sáṃskṛta*.

Over two centuries ago, the spelling was picked up incorrectly by the British and it has continued. This is being rectified by the universities, and it is now called Samskrit.

That said, because the usage of "Sanskrit" is so prevalent, it is not wrong to use it. On this site you might find both spellings used.
