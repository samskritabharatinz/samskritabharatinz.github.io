Namaste, and welcome to the new home of Samskrita Bharati New Zealand.

We have rebuilt our website to make it easier to find a class, keep up with
events, and learn about spoken Sanskrit in Aotearoa. Over the coming weeks we
will be adding more here - stories from our classes, notes on the language, and
news of camps and gatherings.

If you have been thinking about learning Sanskrit, there is no better time.
Our beginner classes assume nothing, start with conversation, and run online so
you can join from anywhere in New Zealand.

To find out more, visit the [Learn](/learn/) page or write to us at
[{{ site.email }}](mailto:{{ site.email }}).
