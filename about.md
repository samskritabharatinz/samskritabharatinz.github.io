Samskrita Bharati New Zealand is a not-for-profit charitable trust, run by
volunteers, dedicated to sharing Samskritam (Sanskrit) as a living, spoken
language. We are the New Zealand chapter of the worldwide Samskrita Bharati
movement, which began in India and now reaches learners in dozens of countries.

Our classes are open to everyone, whatever your background or prior knowledge.
Because most of our teaching happens online, anyone anywhere in New Zealand can
take part - from Kaitaia to Invercargill - on a weekday evening, without leaving
home.

## What we believe

Sanskrit is not only an ancient or ceremonial language. It is a language you can
speak, think in, and enjoy. Our approach starts with conversation, so you are
using the language from your very first class.
