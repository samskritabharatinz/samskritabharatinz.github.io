A few places we recommend - our wider Samskrita Bharati family, and resources our students find useful.

Samskrita Bharati New Zealand on Facebook

Follow us for class news, events, and updates from the community.

Samskrita Bharati India

This is a link to our parent organisation, Samskrita Bharati India

Samskrita Bharati Worldwide

Here you can find links to sister Samskrita Bharati organisations in other parts of the world

Sanskrit Dictionary

A nice and easy way to look up words in English to find the Sanskrit word or vice versa

Sanskrit Documents

This site has a nice collection of various types of Sanskrit documents - be they shlokas, texts or literature

Sanskrit from home (Vyoma)

A great source for learning various things related to Sanskrit - Grammar, Poetry, Texts, Shlokas. They also have a great digital OTT. Register to get access to many free resources.

Ashtadhyayi

A great reference site for Sanskrit students. It contains links to the Dhaatupaatha, Shabdapaatha, Amarakosha, Sutras and much more. A very valuable resource for learners of Sanskrit.
